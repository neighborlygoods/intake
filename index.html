Services Agreement & Scope of Work

About Neighborly Goods and Services LLC

Neighborly Goods and Services LLC is a home economics business operated by an independent consultant who draws on personal experience, research, and community knowledge to help households identify potential areas of savings, income opportunity, and financial efficiency.

This is a neighbor-to-neighbor service — not a financial institution, law firm, accounting practice, or government agency. Your purchase includes a written Home Efficiency Report delivered digitally. Follow-up consultations to discuss your report results are available separately.

"Think of this report the way you'd think of a trusted, resourceful friend sitting across the table from you — someone who has done the research, made the mistakes, and found the opportunities, and genuinely wants to share what they know. That's exactly what this is."

What Your Report Includes

Your Home Efficiency Report is a personalized written document designed to raise your awareness of opportunities you may not know exist. Specifically, you may receive information and suggestions related to:

  • Utility programs, rebates, and rate plan optimizations available in your area
  • Grocery savings strategies, coupon stacking, rewards programs, and shopping approaches
  • Credit card rewards and cash-back opportunities suited to your spending habits
  • Potential income opportunities through side businesses, gig economy, or underutilized assets
  • General awareness of tax-advantaged accounts and commonly overlooked deductions
  • Home efficiency improvements and applicable incentive programs
  • Employer benefits and programs that many people never discover or use
  • Budgeting frameworks, savings strategies, and financial organization approaches

What This Report Does Not Include

It is important that you understand the scope of this service. Your report is a written reference document. Neighborly Goods and Services LLC does not provide:

  • Legal advice of any kind — including advice related to divorce, contracts, or liability
  • Tax preparation, tax filing, or certified tax advice (we are not a CPA or enrolled agent)
  • Licensed financial planning, investment advice, or securities recommendations
  • Insurance advice or brokerage services of any kind
  • Medical, psychological, or healthcare guidance
  • Guarantees of savings, income, or any specific financial outcome

All information in your report is for general awareness and educational purposes only. You are always encouraged to consult a licensed professional — including a CPA, attorney, or Certified Financial Planner — before making significant financial decisions.

Accuracy and Applicability

Recommendations are based on the information you provide and on general knowledge of programs, strategies, and opportunities that exist at the time your report is prepared. Availability of specific programs, rebates, rates, and offers can change. You are responsible for verifying the current availability and terms of any specific recommendation before acting on it.

Dollar estimates provided during the consultation are projections intended to illustrate potential opportunity — they are not guarantees of actual savings or income.

Confidentiality

All information you share in this intake form is used solely for the purpose of preparing your personalized Home Efficiency Report. Your information will not be sold, shared with third parties, or used for marketing purposes. Your financial details are treated with the same care and discretion you would expect from any trusted professional relationship.

Your Autonomy

Every recommendation in your report is a suggestion for you to evaluate on your own terms and timeline. You are under no obligation to act on any recommendation. You know your life, your household, and your priorities better than anyone. This consultation is simply a tool to help you see possibilities you may not have considered — what you do with that information is entirely and always up to you.
